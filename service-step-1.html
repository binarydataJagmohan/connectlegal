CATEGORY NAME

Two-hour Intellectual Property counselling session exclusive for you

Starts from

AED 450

Get your invention/concept assessed by an Intellectual Property (IP) specialist. The IP lawyer you hire will help you comprehend if it is worth the Intellectual Property protection and advise you on the best possible ways of such protection based on your situation.

Service description

Get advice about your real property related issue from an experienced UAE lawyer by phone. Within a 15-minute phone call, the lawyer you hire can answer your questions and give you advice and guidance on topics like:

  • tenant-landlord relations (tenancy contracts, non-payment of rent, rental disputes, eviction procedures, etc.),
  • sale and purchase of real property in the UAE (sale and purchase agreements SPA/MOU, deposits, reservation forms, property registration, etc.),
  • disputes with real estate developers (property handover, termination of SPA, compensation claims, etc.)

This service is right for you if you are a tenant/landlord or buyer/seller of the real property in the UAE, and you need advice and guidance on preventing or resolving legal issues in accordance with UAE laws.

What's included
  • 15-minute phone (or online) call with an attorney. Answers and advice. The lawyer will suggest options for you, based on their experience helping other people with similar issues.
  • Privacy. Everything you discuss with your lawyer is protected by attorney-client privilege.
What's not included
  • Reviewing documents.
  • Long-distance telephone calls. If you are based outside the UAE then probably you will need to call the lawyer yourself, or agree on some other way of voice communication.
  • Privacy. Everything you discuss with your lawyer is protected by attorney-client privilege.
  • Any additional advice or work by the lawyer after the 15-minute advice session.

Most people get the answers they need regarding their legal issues during the call, but if your case requires additional work – for example, you would like to file a case with Rental Dispute Settlement Center or would like to negotiate compensation with the developer – you are welcome to talk to your lawyer about hiring them for extra work.
